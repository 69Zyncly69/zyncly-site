The Founder

Automation built by an operator, for operators

Luke DeMarco has spent his career founding and running businesses in environments where operational failure is simply not an option. As Director of Operations at Hangar 8 — an AIM-listed private jet management company — he coordinated the global movement of aviation assets for ultra-high-net-worth clients across some of the most complex, zero-margin-for-error scenarios in the industry.

He has also built a fleet leasing operation for Uber drivers, founded Sear — a beef brand now doing over £2m in revenue across food truck events and direct-to-consumer BBQ beef — and currently manages a regenerative herd of Aberdeen Angus cattle through Cotswold Cattle Co. Every venture shares the same founding philosophy: strip a complex problem to its simplest form, cut out the clever, and build systems that work without supervision.

Zyncly is where that philosophy meets intelligent automation — applying the same hard-won operational rigour to workflows that most businesses simply accept as an unavoidable cost of doing business.
